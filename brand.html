TETAWAY – ON THE GO

A Vietnamese fast food, ready for the world

Inspired by the story from the time of Nguyễn Huệ, when a soldier carried a piece of bánh tét during his journey, the cake embodied a simple yet powerful idea: compact, portable, and energy-sustaining. Originally created out of necessity, bánh tét became a practical food designed for movement.

In today’s fast-paced world, the demand for convenient, on-the-go meals continues to grow globally. However, many fast food options lack cultural identity and authenticity. This creates an opportunity for something different — a product that is both convenient and meaningful.

TETAWAY redefines bánh tét as a globally adaptable fast food — combining the efficiency of modern convenience food with the richness of Vietnamese culinary heritage. Designed to be compact, easy to store, and suitable for transport, it meets the needs of international markets while maintaining its cultural roots.
